How to make Bullfrog

Ingredients:

  • 1 cucumber
  • 1 green pepper
  • 1 red pepper
  • 3 sprigs celery
  • salt
  • 5g ginger
  • 2g prickly ash
  • 10g huangjiu
  • 3 soft green onions
  • oil
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • dried red peppers
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • basil
  • spicy bean paste
  • 1 tsp sugar

Instructions:

  1. Clean the bullfrog and chop it into 1-centimeter segments cutting through the bone. Soak the meat in a mixture of sliced ginger, prickly ash and huangjiu for 10 minutes.
  2. Smash the garlic. Chop the red pepper, green pepper, dried red pepper, cucumber and green onion. Slice the ginger. Chop the basil very thin.
  3. Oil a pan and stir fry the oil and cook the garlic, dried red pepper and ginger.
  4. Add the bullfrog and continue cooking.
  5. When the bullfrog is cooked, add the spicy bean paste, soy sauce and sugar. Cook over a high flame for at least 1 minute.
  6. Add the celery and cucumber and cook to even out the seasonings.
  7. Add the basil last. Stir and serve.
