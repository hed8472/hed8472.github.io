I watched Wolf Warrior 2, an action movie consisting of a deadly and powerful protagonist who used to be in Chinese special forces. With his career in tatters, China's deadliest Special Forces operative has settled into a quiet life on the sea. But when he crosses paths with a sadistic band of mercenaries terrorizing innocent civilians, he must reaffirm his duty as a soldier and save the day once again. Fists (and bullets, tanks, missiles and much more) will fly in this adrenaline-fueled tour de force of bravura action filmmaking, all culminating into a climatic battle between the Wolf Warrior and the mercenary leader.

I rate this film a 9/10. Although having a good plot, its ending is a little cheesy (not spoiling it for you). It has a lot of action, good chases and action scenes, and often left me wondering what would happen next.
